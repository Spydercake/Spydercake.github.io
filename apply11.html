How to Build Good Credit

What we consider credit is really just a history of succesful (and unsuccesful) transaction that we make in our lives. With this in mind it generally makes sense that in order to build a good credit score, one will need to build a history of good transcations. The following list is what I would recommend someone do if they wish to build a good credit score.

  • Make your payments on time. Bills sent to collections are terrible for your credit history.
  • Two, keep the amount of accounts and the credit utilization on those accounts, low. You want to show a history of smart credit utilization, not a history of large amounts of utilization.
  • Three, keep accounts open for as long as possible. Pay them off but leave them open to show a history.
